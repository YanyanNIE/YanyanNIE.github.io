About this website - 关于网站

As an information engineer in reading, I hope to cultivate my habit of summarizing knowledges in a timely manner through personal website, and improve my abilities in all aspects.

The website was just started in May of 2018, and many functions are still being explored. There is not much summary of knowledges. In addition, the author is still thinking which language as the basic language of the website. Now, i have decided to apply chinese as default language, and will add the corresponding English and French versions step by step.

作为一只在读信息工程师小透明, 希望通过个人网站的形式培养自己及时进行知识点总结的习惯, 以及提高自己各方面的能力.

网站是在18年5月份刚刚开始建立的, 目前很多功能还在摸索中, 知识点的总结也没有太多. 另外, 作者还在踌躇用哪个语言作为网站的基础语言, 目前基本决定以中文为主, 会慢慢增加相应英语和法语的版本.
